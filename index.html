3 · Deliberately NOT indexed — don't panic when these stay out

20 legal pages (noindex, follow) privacy.html · terms.html · disclaimer.html · contact.html — ×5 languages (root, /de/, /da/, /no/, /fr/). They carry the compliance content but are kept out of the index on purpose (standard affiliate hygiene).

4 · GSC setup — one-time, ~3 minutes

  1. Open search.google.com/search-console → Add property → choose URL prefix → enter https://tryretafit.com/.
  2. Verification: pick HTML tag → copy the <meta name="google-site-verification" …> line it shows → send it to me → I put it in the site's head and you press Verify 2 minutes later. (Alternative: any other method works too — I control both the site files and the DNS.)
  3. Left menu → Sitemaps → paste sitemap.xml → Submit.
  4. Start request-indexing in the order below (URL Inspection → paste URL → "Request indexing").
