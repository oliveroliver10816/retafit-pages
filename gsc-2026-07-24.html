Why traffic dropped — and what's actually going on

tryretafit.com · Day 7 live · pulled 24 Jul 2026 · GSC dataState = all

Short answer: the traffic isn't dying — the borrowed half evaporated, on schedule.

Roughly half our clicks last week came from someone else's TV ad campaign (the "Reta 3 / Höhle der Löwen" wave). That ad wave ended, so Google stopped showing us for those searches. The site itself is 100% healthy — every page live, indexed, rich-results valid. Nothing is broken or penalised. What's left is our small, real baseline — and that's where the actual work is.

2 · The cause — a competitor's ad wave collapsed

Root cause  reta-3.com is a separate German weight-loss supplement. It ran a "was it on Die Höhle der Löwen?" ad wave. Because our brand is also "reta…", we ranked in front of those searches by accident — "Reta 3" doesn't even appear on our original pages. When their campaign stopped, that traffic vanished with it:

Ruled out  Not a penalty, not a de-index, not a site outage. All 20 pages return 200 + index,follow, robots is production, rich results 20/20 pass. Coverage is intact.

Strip out the borrowed wave and this is the honest baseline: we are invisible on our own brand name. Every day Google shows "retafit" to 110–150 people, but we sit at position ~7 (below the merchant's own buyretafit.com and other affiliates), so we get 0–2 clicks. Compare: on "reta fit" (with a space) we rank ~3 and pull 25% CTR. Same brand, same intent — the only difference is rank.

And underneath even that: of the ~30 clicks that did reach the store this week, zero bought. Traffic was never the whole problem — rank on our own name and conversion are.

4 · How we make it better

Stop chasing borrowed waves (they always die in weeks). Build assets that are ours.

  1. Win our own brand name "retafit." Highest-value fix by far — 110–150 impr/day handed to competitors at position 7. Levers: brand term front-and-centre in H1/title/first paragraph on every hub, internal exact-"retafit" anchor links, a brand-anchor backlink drip, Indexing-API re-push. Target: top-3 → 20%+ CTR (proven by "reta fit").
    Durable — our brand isn't a fad. This is worth more than any new page.
  2. Build the "reta abnehmen" German cluster. 8 real, live commercial queries (erfahrungen · preis · bestellen · tabletten · nebenwirkungen · dosierung · erfahrungsberichte · deutschland) with zero coverage today. Real non-borrowed demand we can own.
    Germany is now 70% of clicks — deepen it where the intent is commercial, not TV-curiosity.
  3. Fix conversion, not just traffic. Our TV / Reta-3 pages answer the question and let people leave. Move the price + CTA above the informational content and add a bridge to the offer. Honest caveat: the merchant's checkout is what actually closes — 30 clicks / 0 sales may point to a store problem we don't control, worth raising with the network.
    The only lever our pages own is store click-through (DE ~12% today).
  4. Poland — decide the question. "retafit pl" is climbing (8 → 39 impr) with zero PL content, the same signal that made Denmark our best geo. Ask Jennie whether the offer runs in Poland before building.
